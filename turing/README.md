# Turing

Turing.com is an AI-powered platform that matches **pre-vetted, high-caliber remote software developers** with leading U.S. and Silicon Valley companies seeking to fill engineering roles.

Here's a breakdown of its core functions:

*   **For Companies:** Turing offers access to a global pool of elite tech talent rigorously vetted through AI-powered tests, coding challenges, and interviews, ensuring they meet high standards for technical skill, communication, and work ethic. The goal is to help companies quickly hire experienced, full-time remote engineers for long-term engagements, reducing hiring time and cost.
*   **For Developers:** Turing provides opportunities for top developers worldwide to secure long-term, full-time remote positions with competitive salaries, career growth, and the chance to work on cutting-edge projects for top U.S. companies without needing to relocate. Developers must pass a comprehensive vetting process to join Turing's network.

In essence, Turing aims to streamline and democratize the hiring process for high-quality remote tech talent globally, offering a reliable solution for both companies seeking skilled engineers and developers seeking stable, rewarding remote careers.
